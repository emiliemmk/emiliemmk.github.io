ABOUT

I'm currently studying both the bachelor program in Marketing & Brand Management at Kristiania University College and the bachelor program in Interaction Design at the University of Bergen.

Marketing background

I believe in the importance of seeing all meeting points between a company and it's target audience as an opportunity for communication, and see the importance of recognizing that all communication between company and user is part of a bigger marketing process and customer-relationship.

User-centered Design

When designing I always design with the user in mind. Planning the whole design process around the users with research and usability testing as the base is key to good interaction design.
